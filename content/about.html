We help you develop the skills you need to do the stuff you care about.

We start with what you care about, then deliver courses that address your needs.

We're student focussed to ensure we create an environment in which you'll flourish. You won't find us parachuting in to perform PowerPoint Karaoke!

We supply teaching and learning that improves your skills, challenges you to develop and gives you the autonomy to sustain your progress.

We are also currently active developers with up-to-date expertise, links into the wider software development community and track records delivering software in a wide variety of business environments.

Finally, we engage with you to ensure you're getting the best from our training.

What do we Teach?

Broadly, three sorts of things:

  1. Python and its ecosystem:
    • Core Python skills and the standard library (from first steps with Python to advanced subjects such as asyncio);
    • Data science (pandas, IPython, etc);
    • Web development (including frameworks such as Django, Flask and Twisted);
    • Scraping and data processing (with modules like Beautiful Soup, lxml and Scrapy); and
    • Python and the Internet of Things (such as building APIs for IoT and MicroPython).
  2. Software sustainability within an enterprise setting:
    • Tools of the trade;
    • Pair programming;
    • Knowledge transfer; and
    • Working within a business.
  3. Software development methodologies and practices:
    • test driven development;
    • version control;
    • deployment; and
    • monitoring.

How do we Deliver?

We're teach using a blend of different styles to suit both the subject matter and students. This may range from simple exercises, pair programming, dojo style group work, peer-review, self assessment and, of course, good old chalk'n'talk.

We make our classes simple and easy to follow and cover subjects from the simplest to most complex. We never make things more complicated than they need to be and we're atuned to our student's backgrounds so we can work with them to improve from their current situation.

Finally, we work hard to place our training into a context that is meaningful for our students. This is important because we believe we succeed when our graduates have the confidence to continue to learn and develop autonomously within their working environment.
